Interpretation
--------------
1. SpecAugment+Mixup HURTS Vanilla CNN: removing both augmentations improves
   accuracy by +2.96% (89.75% vs 86.79% +/- 3.46%) but the +2.96% delta sits
   inside the 3-seed std band, so the difference is not significant.
2. SpecAugment+Mixup HELPS DS-CNN modestly: -2.21% without augmentation.
3. SpecAugment+Mixup HELPS BC-ResNet substantially: -10.43% without
   augmentation. BC-ResNet relies most heavily on the augmentation regime.

Notes
-----
- WITHOUT runs use a single seed (42) only; no std band is available.
- The "WITH" std bands are sample stdev across 3 seeds; on n=3 the bands are
  wide and should be interpreted as rough scale, not strict significance.
