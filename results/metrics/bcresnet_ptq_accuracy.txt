BC-ResNet Quantization Comparison
==================================================

Post-Training Quantization (PTQ):
  Representative dataset: 200 samples from training split
  Quantized model: models/tflite/bc_resnet_ptq.tflite
  Model size: 97.34 KB
  Test accuracy: 80.61%

Quantization-Aware Training (QAT) [current]:
  Same model converted to int8 after 15 extra training epochs
  Test accuracy: 81.16% (from bootstrap_predictions.npz)

Difference: -0.01 pp (PTQ vs QAT)

Note: BC-ResNet received 15 additional training epochs during QAT.
Other architectures (DS-CNN, Vanilla) used only PTQ (no extra training).
This comparison demonstrates the impact of training method on int8 accuracy.
